3) Joint acceleration limits
ddq_max = [15.0, 7.5, 10.0, 12.5, 15.0, 20.0, 20.0] rad/s^2

Per joint:
q1_ddot_max: 15.0 rad/s^2
q2_ddot_max: 7.5 rad/s^2
q3_ddot_max: 10.0 rad/s^2

q5_ddot_max: 15.0 rad/s^2
q6_ddot_max: 20.0 rad/s^2

4) Cartesian limits
translation velocity max:    1.7 m/s
rotation velocity max:       2.5 rad/s
elbow velocity max:          2.1750 rad/s

translation acceleration max: 13.0 m/s^2
rotation acceleration max:    25.0 rad/s^2
elbow acceleration max:       10.0 rad/s^2

5) Notes
- Joint 4 is always negative and does not reach 0.
- Joint 6 is asymmetric and mostly positive.
- For safety, Franka also uses position-dependent velocity limits near joint boundaries.
